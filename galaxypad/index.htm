Welcome to the Galaxy
A Decentralized Infrastructure for NFT Games and Metaverse Projects
GalaxyPad is a decentralized cross-chain infrastructure empowering project owners to launch NFT games and Metaverse projects.
The platform offers guaranteed fair-levels for exclusive access to NFT, GameFi and Metaverse IDO.
We strongly believe in the future of the Metaverse and our team is fully ready to launch the next-generation tools for NFT games and Metaverse projects.

Features Overview

  • Decentralized Launchpad
  • Planet Incubation
  • Planet Marketplace
  • Planet Farm
  • Galaxy Kingdoms P2E (more details to follow)
  • PlanetDAO
